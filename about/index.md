I'm a software engineer with a background in academic research. I completed my Bachelors in Computer Engineering in Singapore and then pursued a PhD in Computer Science at the University of St Andrews, Scotland, where I published work on mobile networking, simulation, and remote health monitoring.

These days I build things with **Django**, **React**, and **Python**. I write about software engineering practices, infrastructure, and the occasional book review on this site. Topics I keep coming back to include test-driven development, self-hosting, and how to ship software that lasts.

I'm interested in Machine Learning, Simulations, and Complexity.

You can find me on [GitHub](https://github.com/devanr) or browse my [publications](/publications/).

I'm open to contract and freelance work — feel free to [get in touch](mailto:rehunathan@gmail.com).
